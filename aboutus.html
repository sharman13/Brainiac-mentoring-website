About Us


HomefreshbySheetal is conceptulize by Sheetal Maheshwari who is passionate about cooking and baking. She started as a Home Baker and delivering the cakes on order and over the time develop her self as a professional chef. Now in her new journey she designed Bite Size online cooking courses which are available at very affordable price with highest standard of quality.

These courses are designed very effectively and user friendly. The good response and curiosity about these courses among women across the globe is giving thumbs up to idea of these courses.

The vision of Sheetal Maheshwarii is to develop numerous home baker across India to help them out to overcome difficulties faced by them and taught them right skills to succed in their life.
